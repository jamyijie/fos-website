Milo’s Story: A Gentle Soul Looking for Love

Milo is a sweet Ragdoll who was found hiding under a neighbor’s porch, too shy and scared to come out. His fur was matted, and he was hungry and tired when we first rescued him. With gentle care and patience, Milo has slowly started to trust again. He loves curling up in warm laps and has the softest purr that can brighten any room. Milo is ready to find a loving family who will give him the patience and kindness he deserves. If you’re looking for a gentle companion to welcome into your home, Milo might just be the perfect match.
